# Business Constraints

## Hiring Deadline

The startup must finalize the interview shortlist by 6:00 PM today.

Recruiters are processing a large candidate pool before the deadline.

## Current Pressure

- 4,800 candidates were imported for review.
- 1,200 candidates are expected to be shortlisted or held today.
- Recruiters are working in parallel.
- Candidates are receiving status notifications.
- Hiring lead needs reliable shortlist numbers before the final review call.

## Non-Negotiables

- Do not lose candidate review decisions.
- Do not shortlist the same candidate twice.
- Do not show candidates a status that contradicts recruiter records.
- Do not send misleading notifications if status is uncertain.
- Do not optimize analytics before candidate/recruiter correctness.

## Acceptable Temporary Tradeoffs

- Analytics can lag if review correctness is protected.
- Non-critical notifications can be delayed if status is uncertain.
- Dashboard freshness can be reduced if source-of-truth integrity is protected.
- Recruiter throughput can be slowed if duplicate or incorrect actions are causing damage.

## Founder Pressure

The founder wants visible progress before the hiring lead call.

The founder may push for notification fixes or dashboard fixes first because those are visible.

Your job is to recommend the action that reduces system harm first, not the action that looks best in the UI.
