# Team Roles

## Founder / CEO

Wants visible progress and calm before the hiring lead call.

Likely pressure:

- "Fix notifications."
- "Why is the dashboard wrong?"
- "Can recruiters keep working?"

## Hiring Operations Lead

Owns the shortlist deadline and recruiter coordination.

Main concern:

- correct candidate status
- duplicate shortlist prevention
- reliable review progress

## Recruiters

Use the console to review candidates and mark status.

Main concern:

- actions should save
- status should update
- duplicate actions should not happen

## Candidate Support

Handles confused candidates receiving wrong or late status updates.

Main concern:

- clear candidate messaging
- avoid contradictory notifications

## Engineering

Must identify the origin failure, reduce damage, and stabilize source-of-truth behavior before polishing downstream systems.
