# Startup Context

## Company

TalentBridge is a fast-growing hiring operations startup helping companies review engineering candidates from colleges and online cohorts.

## Current Event

TalentBridge is running a deadline-heavy candidate review sprint for a partner company.

The hiring team must finalize interview shortlists by 6:00 PM.

Recruiters are reviewing thousands of candidate profiles in parallel.

## Why The Incident Matters

If review actions are wrong or inconsistent:

- candidates may receive incorrect status updates
- recruiters may duplicate shortlist actions
- hiring leads may see wrong dashboard counts
- operations may lose trust in the console
- interview scheduling may start from bad data

## Founder Assumption

The founder sees delayed notifications and dashboard mismatch and says:

> "This looks like a notification or recruiter action issue. Fix that first."

Your task is to decide whether that assumption is right, partially right, or misleading.
