# Submission Guide

Submit only the lean Day 2 pack.

## Required Submission

1. Incident Reasoning Brief
2. 4-minute System Defense Video

Founder may schedule a live viva after review.

## Discord / Cohort Submission Format

```md
# Day 2 Submission

Name:
College:
Branch / Year:
Variant ID:

Incident Reasoning Brief:
System Defense Video:

One-line realization:
I initially thought ________, but the evidence showed ________.
```

## Submission Quality Standard

Short, evidence-heavy answers beat long essays.

Your submission must show:

- founder assumption challenged
- origin failure identified with confidence
- at least two hypotheses evaluated
- symptom vs root cause separation
- failure propagation chain
- first 15-minute action
- delayed actions
- tradeoff ownership
- AI / assumption verification

## Hard Fail Signals

- generic architecture essay
- no evidence citations
- buzzwords without proof
- treating first visible symptom as root cause
- no delayed-work reasoning
- no AI / assumption verification
